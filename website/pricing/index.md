# Pricing

**Outcome-Based Pricing for Engineering-Led Marketing.**

We don't bill by the hour—that creates misaligned incentives. Our pricing is structured around outcomes: infrastructure built, systems deployed, and results measured.

We offer three engagement models depending on your needs:

| Model | Structure | Best For |
|-------|-----------|----------|
| **Growth Ops Retainer** | Flat monthly fee | Ongoing GTM management and optimization |
| **Performance-Based** | Base + variable per qualified meeting | Outbound-focused engagements |
| **Infrastructure Projects** | One-time fee | Building specific systems (enrichment pipelines, CRM setup) |

## Frequently Asked Questions

### How is pricing structured?
We offer three models: **Growth Ops Retainers** (flat monthly fee for ongoing work), **Performance-Based** (base + variable per qualified meeting for outbound), and **Infrastructure Projects** (one-time fee for building specific systems).

### What's included in setup fees?
Setup fees cover tool implementation (Clay, CRM, outbound platforms), integration configuration, DNS infrastructure for deliverability, custom workflow development, and initial strategy. You own all infrastructure we build—no vendor lock-in.

### Do you require long-term contracts?
Initial engagements are typically 3-month commitments to allow proper implementation and optimization cycles. After the initial period, we move to month-to-month with 30-day notice.

### What if we only need one service?
Every package can be scoped individually. Book a discovery call and we'll build a custom proposal.

### What are your payment terms?
Monthly retainers are billed at the start of each month. Setup fees are 50% upfront, 50% at completion. We accept ACH, wire, and major credit cards.
