## Calculators

### CAC Calculator
Calculate your fully-loaded Customer Acquisition Cost.

**Inputs:**
- Marketing spend (monthly)
- Sales team cost (monthly)
- Tools & software cost (monthly)
- New customers acquired (monthly)

**Outputs:**
- Fully-loaded CAC
- Marketing CAC vs. Sales CAC breakdown
- Benchmark comparison (Seed / Series A / Series B)

### Burn Rate Calculator
Model your runway under different scenarios.

**Inputs:**
- Current cash balance
- Monthly operating expenses
- Monthly revenue (if any)
- Expected changes (hiring, cuts)

**Outputs:**
- Months of runway
- Date of cash-out
- Scenario models (conservative, moderate, aggressive)

[Use Burn Rate Calculator →](#calculator-burn)

### SEO ROI Estimator
Estimate the pipeline impact of organic traffic investment.

**Inputs:**
- Target keywords and search volume
- Estimated ranking position
- Average conversion rate
- Average deal size

**Outputs:**
- Projected organic traffic
- Projected leads
- Projected pipeline value
- SEO payback period
