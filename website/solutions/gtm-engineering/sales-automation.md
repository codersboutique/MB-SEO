**Your Sales Team Shouldn't Be Data Entry Clerks.**

The average sales rep spends only 33% of their time selling. The rest? Updating CRMs, finding phone numbers, and writing follow-up emails. **Sales Automation** removes the admin burden so your expensive talent can focus on closing revenue.

## The Problem: CRM Chaos

"If it's not in Salesforce, it didn't happen." But nobody puts it in Salesforce. Manual data entry is slow, inaccurate, and hated by sales teams. [**Automation**](/solutions/gtm-engineering/tech-stack/) fixes this.

| Common Mistake | Why It Fails |
|----------------|--------------|
| **Dirty Data** | Duplicate contacts, missing fields, and outdated info make your CRM useless. |
| **Lead Leakage** | Inbound leads sit for hours before being assigned. Speed to lead is critical. |
| **Manual Follow-up** | "Just checking in" emails waste time and get low response rates. |

**The reality:** You are paying high salaries for low-value work. Automation fixes the ROI equation.

## Our Automation Services

### 1. CRM Architecture & Hygiene
We build CRMs ([**HubSpot/Salesforce**](/solutions/gtm-engineering/tech-stack/)) that maintain themselves.

**What We Build:**
- **Auto-Enrichment:** New records are instantly populated with LinkedIn data, company size, and revenue.
- **De-duplication Logic:** Automated merging of duplicate records.
- **Activity Logging:** syncing email and calendar activity without manual input.

### 2. Speed-to-Lead Routing
We ensure every lead is touched instantly.

**What We Build:**
- **Round-Robin Assignment:** Fair distribution of leads based on logic (territory, size, availability).
- **Instant SMS/Email Acknowledgment:** "Human-like" immediate responses to inbound requests.
- **Slack/Teams Alerts:** Pinging reps instantly when a high-value prospect engages.

### 3. Deal Velocity Automation
We automate the stages of the deal cycle.

**What We Build:**
- **Automated Nurture:** Prospects who "ghost" are automatically entered into long-term nurture sequences.
- **Contract Automation:** Generating NDAs and contracts with one click.
- **Renewal Reminders:** Alerts 90 days before contract expiry.
