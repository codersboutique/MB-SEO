## Frequently Asked Questions

### How is AI local SEO different from traditional local SEO?
Traditional local SEO focuses on keyword rankings and citation building. AI local SEO optimizes for **semantic matching** (does AI understand what you do?), **conversational queries** (how people actually ask), and **cross-platform trust verification** (does AI believe your data?).

### What is the AI local pack?
The AI local pack is the set of 3-5 business recommendations shown by AI assistants when users ask for local services. Unlike traditional Maps that show 10+ results, AI curates a small list based on trust signals and semantic match.

### How long does it take to see results?
Directory sync improvements appear within 2-4 weeks. AI local pack visibility typically improves within 60-90 days as trust signals compound.
