## The Enterprise Challenge

Enterprise-stage companies face a different set of strategic imperatives:
- **Defend the moat:** Competitors are copying your playbook. You need differentiation at every touchpoint.
- **Multi-product complexity:** Launching and marketing multiple products to overlapping audiences.
- **Global expansion:** Localizing GTM for new markets without fragmenting the brand.
- **Upmarket motion:** Moving from SMB/Mid-Market to true Enterprise deals with long sales cycles.

## Our Enterprise Solutions

### 1. Multi-Product GTM Architecture
When you have multiple products, your GTM stack becomes a system of systems.

**What We Build:**
- **Unified Data Model:** A single source of truth for customer data across all products.
- **Cross-Sell/Upsell Workflows:** Automated triggers that identify expansion opportunities based on usage patterns.
- **Product-Specific Messaging Engines:** Dynamic content that adapts based on which product the prospect is exploring.

### 2. Global SEO & AEO Localization
Expanding internationally means more than translation. It requires local entity authority.

**What We Build:**
- **Hreflang & International Schema:** Technical SEO for multi-language, multi-region sites.
- **Local Entity Establishment:** Creating local Wikidata entries, Google Business Profiles, and regional press coverage.
- **Regional Keyword Mapping:** Understanding how search intent differs by market.

### 3. ABM Orchestration at Scale
Enterprise sales require precision. We integrate your GTM data with advertising for surgical targeting.

**What We Build:**
- [**Account-Based Retargeting**](/solutions/paid-user-acquisition/linkedin/): Integration of website visitor data with LinkedIn Matched Audiences.
- **Buying Committee Mapping:** Multi-threaded outreach to all stakeholders within target accounts.
- **Intent Data Integration:** Using Bombora, G2, or 6sense to prioritize accounts showing active buying signals.

### 4. Category Design & Narrative Control
The winner writes the rules. We help you define the category.

**What We Build:**
- **Analyst Relations Strategy:** Briefings and positioning for Gartner, Forrester, and emerging AI-focused analysts.
- **Original Research Programs:** Quarterly reports that establish you as the industry data source.
- **Executive Thought Leadership:** Ghostwriting, speaking engagements, and LinkedIn presence for your C-suite.
